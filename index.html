Font size

Adjust the size of text to make it larger or smaller for better readability.

Text spacing

Modify the space between characters for improved legibility.

Line height

Adjust the vertical spacing between lines of text for enhanced readability.

Text align

Change the alignment of text to left, right or center.

Highlight links

Make clickable links more noticeable with highlighting.

Dyslexia font

Enable a font designed to assist individuals with dyslexia in reading.

Contrast

Modify the background and text contrast to improve visibility.

Smart contrast

Automatically adjust contrast for optimal readability.

High saturation

Increase color saturation for better visibility.

Monochrome

Convert content to black and white for reduced visual distractions.

Hide images

Disable images to focus on text content.

Pause animations

Stop animations to reduce distractions and improve accessibility.

Reading guide

Highlight and guide through text for easier reading.

Large cursor

Increase the cursor size for better visibility.

Speak text

Have the content read aloud for those with visual impairments or reading difficulties.
